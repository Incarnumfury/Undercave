Game Description:

Name: Undercave

Description: You are an adventurous dwarf who craves the riches of a long forgotten tomb. However the tomb is guarded by many dangerous foes and creatures. With the aid of a wizards enchantment you are able to die and ressurect stronger than before and retain a vague sense of knowledge you gained from before to defeat the creatures that reside within the tomb. How many deaths will it take for you to plunder the riches of the UNDERCAVE?
